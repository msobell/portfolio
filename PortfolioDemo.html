  1. Type your name where it says "type your name"
  2. Press "Set Name"
  3. Press "Start"
  4. Adjust the sliders
  5. Press "Send"
    The server now has your gambles and you must hit "Play" (or wait for the architect to hit play).
    You can see how you did by the numbers above the gambles which correspond to the sliders.
    IMPORTANT: The sliders are normalized.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5.
  7. Good luck!
